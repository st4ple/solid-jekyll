Vision

To promote excellence in teaching, research and service in Science and Mathematics, developing the critical abilities of students through the study of Science.

Objectives

  • To cooperate with other Faculties in the training of various professionals, e.g. Bachelor of Education (B.Ed), Science and B.Ed, Secondary.
  • To provide a firm Year 1 (level 100 courses) foundation in the Basic Sciences for those students who transfer to other faculties.
  • To provide a firm intellectual base for those students who are designated for transfer to other institutions to take program which are not offered in this university e.g. Veterinary Science, Pharmacy etc.

Mission

To help students become responsible members of society through their education and proficiency in the various branches of Science.
