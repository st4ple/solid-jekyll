PROGRAMMES AND TITLES


The Department of Computer Science offers the following undergraduate and postgraduate programmes:
Single major programme leading to the award of:

  • BSc(Computer Science)
  • BSc(Computing with Finance)
  • BSc(Information Technology)
  • BSc(Computer Information Systems)

Combined minor/major programme(Computer Science major)

  • B.Sc.(other subject Major/Computer Science Minor)

Masters of Science

  • MSc Computer Science
  • MSc Computer Information Systems

MPhil

  • Masters of Philosophy in Computer Science
  • Masters of Philosophy in Computer Information Systems

PhD

  • Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science
  • Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Information Systems
