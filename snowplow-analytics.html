Why Snowplow Analytics?

Snowplow is the Open Source digital analytics events pipeline that captures and organises everything as it happens in real time. Using modern data processing and storage techniques, Snowplow allows you to record everything and keep the raw, original source data forever so you can always try a new analysis in the future.

Your data, stored in your systems

Snowplow is self-hosted so you keep control of the data and how it’s used. Store sensitive data in your AWS (Amazon Web Services) account using your existing security controls and join it with your existing data sets without having to jump through hoops.

Massive transaction volumes

Snowplow scales to your event throughput events per day, in real-time, without missing a beat using reliable, highly available AWS services. The costs of data processing and storage are remarkably affordable even at high volumes.

Build analytics into your product

Companies around the world leverage Snowplow’s pre-built analytics capabilities to embed analytics functions into their customer-facing products without having to build it all from scratch. Snowplow’s Open Source model means you can economically build it into your product without future cost surprises.

Snowplow Managed Services





Security

Snowplow Insights uses Amazon Web Services (AWS) as its storage service, your data never leaves your AWS account. AWS has been accredited under ISO 27001.

Support

As a managed services customer, you have access to support engineering on a follow the sun model. We are available on a 24/7 every day of the year.

Monitoring

All Snowplow Insight services are set up with operations and support ensuring managed services customers focus on building their business, not managing infrastructure.

Scalability

Snowplow Insights is designed to scale in the billions. As you grow, so does the analytics infrastructure to support your business. With managed services, we handle all the autoscaling for you.

Reliability

Snowplow Insights is lossless data by design. No event is trashed. With managed services, rest assured even data with errors will be corrected and stored back in the main tables.

Flexibility

Snowplow Insights is a context rich platform enabling customers to create custom contexts that describe the events that matter. As a managed services customer, we help you build your company schema registry.
